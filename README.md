**Joystick:**

- 5V to 5V
- VRX to A0
- VRY to A1
- SW to 53


**LCD I2C:**
- GND to GND
- 5V to 5V
- SDA to SDA (20)
- SCL to SCL (21)


**Buzzer:**
- GND to GND
- 5V to 11
